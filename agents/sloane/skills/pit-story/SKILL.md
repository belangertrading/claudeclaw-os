# Pit Story — Josh Belanger

## Why This Is Untouchable Content

No 25-year-old influencer has these stories. No MBA has this experience. The Chicago trading pits are gone. Josh was there at 19. That's his unfair advantage — forged in hand-to-hand combat capitalism.

This is documentary-level content. Uncopyable. Gets stronger with time.

## The Format

**Pit moment (specific, vivid) → Lesson that still applies today → Connect to current trading**

Keep it punchy. The pit story sets the hook. The lesson is the payoff. Don't over-explain.

Read `/home/clawdbot/clawd/skills/pit-story/references/examples.md` for proven story structures and the series framework.

## Voice

- First-person, present-tense energy
- Specific details — the 300-pound guy, the exact dollar amount, the year
- The lesson lands at the end, never at the start
- End on the connection to today, not a moral or advice
- Short. These aren't essays. The punch matters more than the length.

## Series Name

**"Trading Pit Lessons That Still Work"**

Each post stands alone. Together they build the origin mythology. Archive of stories that compounds over time.
