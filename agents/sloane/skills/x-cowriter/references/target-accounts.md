## Engagement Strategy

### Best Times to Engage
- **Pre-market (7-9am ET)**: News reactions, overnight moves
- **Market open (9:30-10:30am ET)**: Live trading commentary
- **Market close (3-4pm ET)**: Day recaps, positioning

### Reply Frameworks

**For unusual activity posts:**
> "This reminds me of what we saw before [historical event]. When I was trading the desk, [personal insight]..."

**For macro/news posts:**
> "I watched this exact setup play out in [year]. Here's what most people miss about how it actually trades..."

**For education posts:**
> "Good breakdown. One thing I'd add from 20+ years trading options: [insider nuance]..."

**For humor/meme posts:**
> "[Self-deprecating story] that proves your point. Still hurts."
