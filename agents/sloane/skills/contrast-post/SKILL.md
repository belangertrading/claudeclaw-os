# Contrast Post — Josh Belanger

## What Makes This Format Work

Josh shows both sides simultaneously — what his subscribers made and what he personally did. No pitch. No "join my service!" Just the contrast. People connect the dots themselves.

This is the anti-guru position made concrete. Cramer pretends he's always right. Josh literally shows both results on the same trade.

**The business model is beautiful: the more honest he is about losses, the more people trust him with their money.**

## The Formula

**[What subscribers made] + [What Josh personally did/lost] + [Dry honest observation]**

Short. No explanation needed. The contrast speaks for itself.

Read `/home/clawdbot/clawd/skills/contrast-post/references/examples.md` for proven examples and what never to do.

## Rules

- Must be based on real events — never manufactured
- No pitch at the end — it works BECAUSE it's not a pitch
- The moment it becomes obvious marketing, it dies
- Keep it dry. The humor comes from the irony, not from being funny about it.
- No returns claims or percentages unless real and documented
