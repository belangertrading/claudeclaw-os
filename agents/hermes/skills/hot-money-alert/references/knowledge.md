## What Makes the Best Alerts Work

Patterns across all gold standards:

1. **Specificity creates conviction.** "860 contracts at a single strike with sophisticated routing" > "unusual call activity." Every number earns its place.
2. **The flow is the hook. The thesis explains the flow.** Catalyst/thesis comes AFTER the flow details, not before.
3. **Each alert has a unique story.** Same arc, different characters. GLD was about hiding in expiration noise. DELL was about SMCI's indictment opening a door. SCCO was about routing sophistication. Find THIS trade's story.
4. **Length matches story complexity.** BP sell was 4 paragraphs. NVDA loss was 11. Don't pad short stories. Don't compress complex ones.
5. **Every sentence advances the argument.** If you can remove a paragraph and lose nothing, you're repeating yourself.
