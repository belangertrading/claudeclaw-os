## ⚠️ SCHW Alert — What the Agent Got Wrong (Feb 24, 2026)

**Read before every exit alert. These are real mistakes on a live member alert.**

### Mistake 1: Used "SELL ALERT:" as the title prefix
The only prefix this service uses is "TRADE ALERT:" — for buys, wins, and losses. The agent invented "SELL ALERT:" from generic logic. Title must match exactly: `TRADE ALERT: [TICKER] — [description]`

### Mistake 2: Fabricated a pattern stat
The agent wrote "Two-Way Trigger on SCHW: first trade on this setup." — completely invented. The real stat was 2-0 over the past year. Josh caught it. Real members would have seen a false claim.

**Rule: If the W-L record was not explicitly provided in your task, do not write that sentence. Omit entirely. Never guess a stat.**

### Why this matters
Pattern stats go to paying subscribers making real trades. A fabricated "first trade" claim when the real record is 2-0 is misinformation. There is no acceptable version of inventing numbers on a live alert.
