### Narrative Arc for OI BUY Alerts

**HEADLINE:** `TRADE ALERT: [TICKER] [Pattern] Just Fired — [X]% Pattern`

**OPENER** — Tension, contrast, or surprise. NOT a company description. Each alert finds its own angle:
- "Everyone's betting against Tesla. The Bull Surge says they're wrong."
- "This coal stock just triggered our 719% pattern — for the second time."
- "JPM is setting up for something big."
- "Wall Street is sleeping on BAC. Not for long."

**PATTERN INTRO** — Price + pattern fired + company context + stats:
- "[TICKER] is at $X and our [Pattern] just fired — the one that catches stocks [pattern description]."
- Company context: 1-2 sentences matching THIS trade (mispricing / hidden catalyst / re-trigger / obscure ticker)
- "X-Y record over past X years with X% total returns. Last trigger: [date]. Hit TP [X] days later."
- "It just triggered again." (or variation)

**STOCK CHART** — After "It just triggered again."

**[CUSTOM BOLD HEADLINE]** — The story in one line. NOT "The Fundamentals Support the Pattern." Each alert gets its own:
- "Musk Just Killed Two Cars to Build Robots"
- "Coal to Gas — The Market Doesn't Buy It"
- "The Sell-the-News Trap"

**THESIS** — 3-4 short paragraphs. No bullets. One narrative thread. Specific facts, names, dollar amounts. This is the research layer — find the non-obvious catalyst that makes the pattern timing feel inevitable.

**TIE-IN** — One sentence bridging thesis to action.

**ACTION** — Bold. Full contract. TP/SL from pattern table. Optional spread alternative.

**CLOSING** — Days to exp. Pattern conviction. "Get in, get the X%, get out."

**"What This Trade Looks Like"** — Section heading. Options analysis screenshot.

## What Makes OI Alerts Different from HMT/48HC

1. **Pattern-driven, not flow-driven.** The pattern IS the conviction. No dollar amounts of institutional flow.
2. **Custom bold headlines.** Each alert gets a unique story headline — not "Here's what caught my attention" (HMT) or "The 48-Hour Window Just Opened" (48HC).
3. **Historical pattern stats.** W-L record, total returns %, last trigger date and result. These are the service's edge.
4. **"Get in, get the X%, get out."** OI's signature closer.
5. **"What This Trade Looks Like"** at the end with options screenshot (buy alerts only).
6. **Spread alternatives.** Often offered after the main ACTION line.
