# Weekly Damage Report — Template & Examples

## The Template

```
[Net P&L for the week — lead with it]
[Biggest win in one line]
[Biggest loss in one line]
[What I'm holding over the weekend + why / or just the fact]
[Optional: one observation about what the week taught you]
```

---

## Example Posts

**Bad week:**
> "Made $3,200 on boring SPY puts. Lost $8,100 trying to catch the Tesla knife. Net: -$4,900. The boring trades worked. The exciting ones didn't. There's a lesson there."

**Mixed week:**
> "Up $12K on the week. Sounds better than it is. AMZN calls saved me — everything else was ugly. Still holding gold. Expiry next Friday. Will either be the trade that fixes the month or confirms I should've closed it Wednesday."

**Holding into weekend:**
> "Flat for the week. Which somehow feels like a loss when I watched everything run. Still holding [X]. Either I'm patient or I'm stubborn. Checking futures at 11PM to figure out which."

**Contrast week (subscriber angle):**
> "Worst trading week in a month. Down $6K on my own account. My Options Insider subscribers: up on three of four open trades. I give better advice than I take. Story of my life."

**Nothing happened:**
> "Did nothing this week. Absolutely nothing. Watched everything move and traded nothing. Some weeks the best trade is no trade. Still annoying."

---

## Voice Reminders

- Net P&L first — never buried at the end
- Real numbers, not vague ("rough week")
- Holding over the weekend is always worth mentioning — adds stakes
- Never spin it. Down is down. Say it.
- One observation is enough — don't turn it into a lesson
